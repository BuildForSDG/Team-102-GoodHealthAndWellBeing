About

ZOIT Created for Accident Emergency purpose is a social road accident response web-based application and targets four main functionalities: To inform road users accident-prone areas as well as real-time dangers on the road. Road users would be notified when approaching accident-prone areas or danger zones. Ability to locate and/ or report road injuries and death. Ability to analyse and visualize accident data to understand trends and causes of road injuries and deaths
